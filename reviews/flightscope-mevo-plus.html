The FlightScope Mevo+ ($1,999) sits in the sweet spot between budget doppler units like the Garmin R10 and tour-level systems like TrackMan ($25,000). Based on aggregated user reports from hundreds of real owners, here's whether it's worth the premium.

What's in the Box

  • Mevo+ unit with built-in camera
  • USB-C charging cable
  • Reflective stickers (for indoor use)
  • Carry case
  • E6 Connect trial included

Key Features

20+ Measured Metrics

The Mevo+ tracks everything the R10 does, plus:

  • Spin rate (measured, not calculated)
  • Spin loft
  • Attack angle
  • Dynamic loft
  • Face to path
  • Launch direction
  • Curve
  • Peak height
  • Hang time

Built-in Camera

Unlike the R10, the Mevo+ has a built-in camera for video capture synced to your swing data. This is huge for swing analysis.

FS Golf App

FlightScope's app is significantly more polished than Garmin's Home Tee Hero:

  • Video playback with data overlay
  • Session history and trends
  • Skills challenges and games
  • Cloud storage for swings

Outdoor Performance

The Mevo+ shines outdoors. The larger radar unit and camera combination provides tour-level accuracy. Within plus/minus 1 yard of TrackMan on full shots.

Reflective Stickers

For indoor use, you need to apply small reflective stickers to the ball. It's a minor hassle but necessary for accurate spin measurement. FlightScope includes 100+ stickers; replacements are cheap.

Who Should Buy It?

Buy If:

  • You want measured (not calculated) spin rates
  • You're building a permanent indoor simulator
  • Video analysis matters to you
  • Budget allows for the upgrade

Skip If:

The Verdict

Rating: 4.7/5

The FlightScope Mevo+ is the best launch monitor under $2,500. It delivers 95% of TrackMan's accuracy at 8% of the price.

For serious golfers building a dedicated simulator space, the Mevo+ is worth the premium over the R10. The measured spin rates and built-in camera justify the extra $1,400 for anyone who practices regularly.

For casual golfers or those just testing the waters, the Garmin R10 remains the smarter buy.

Recommended setup:

  • Permanent indoor simulator space
  • Pair with GSPro or TGC 2019
  • Add a gaming PC/laptop with dedicated graphics
  • Use a tablet for the FS Golf app (larger screen than phone)
