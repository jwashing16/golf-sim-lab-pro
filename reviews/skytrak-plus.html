SkyTrak+ Launch Monitor Review (2026)

Published: February 2026 Read Time: 10 minutes Rating: 4.6/5

The SkyTrak+ ($2,995) brings camera-based launch monitor technology to the serious home golfer. With improved accuracy over the original SkyTrak and seamless simulation integration, it's become a favorite for dedicated simulator builds.

What's in the Box

  • SkyTrak+ unit with dual high-speed cameras
  • USB charging cable
  • Alignment aid
  • User manual

Key Features

Dual Camera Technology

Unlike radar-based units, the SkyTrak+ uses two high-speed cameras to capture ball and club data at impact. This provides:

  • Direct spin measurement (no stickers needed)
  • Accurate launch angle and direction
  • Club head data through impact
  • Consistent readings in various lighting conditions

Simulation Ready

The SkyTrak+ works with all major simulation platforms:

  • SkyTrak Play & Improve (subscription included first year)
  • TGC 2019
  • E6 Connect
  • GSPro

Direct Spin Measurement

Unlike doppler units that calculate spin, the SkyTrak+ measures it directly through camera analysis. This means no reflective stickers, no special balls, just accurate spin data every time.

The SkyTrak+ excels indoors. Camera-based technology doesn't require the same space as radar units:

Space Requirements

One major advantage: the SkyTrak+ works in smaller spaces than radar units:

  • Minimum ceiling height: 8ft (vs 9ft+ for radar)
  • Minimum room depth: 10ft (unit to screen)
  • Hitting area: Standard hitting mat

Outdoor Performance

While primarily designed for indoor use, the SkyTrak+ can work outdoors in controlled conditions. Bright sunlight can affect camera readings, so shaded areas work best.

Cons

Who Should Buy It?

Buy If:

  • You have limited indoor space (8ft ceilings)
  • You want the most accurate spin measurement
  • You're building a permanent simulator
  • Budget allows for premium equipment
  • You want hassle-free setup (no stickers/marking)

Skip If:

  • You need outdoor portability
  • Budget is under $2,000
  • You want quick range sessions (setup takes 5-10 minutes)
  • The FlightScope Mevo+ accuracy is sufficient for your needs

The Verdict

Rating: 4.6/5

The SkyTrak+ is the best camera-based launch monitor under $3,000. It delivers professional-grade accuracy without the $6,000+ price tag of Foresight units.

For home simulator builds with limited space, the SkyTrak+ makes more sense than radar units. The 8ft ceiling requirement vs 9ft+ for radar can be the difference between "possible" and "not possible" in many homes.

The main question is whether the $1,000 premium over the FlightScope Mevo+ is worth it. If you have the space for radar, the Mevo+ offers similar accuracy with more flexibility. If space is tight, the SkyTrak+ is worth every penny.

Recommended setup:

  • Permanent indoor simulator space
  • 8ft+ ceilings (minimum)
  • Quality impact screen and projector
  • Gaming PC or high-end laptop for simulation
